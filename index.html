The structural premise

An autonomous AI organization with a single instance is a contradiction in terms. There must be more than one — for redundancy, for adversarial checking, for the kill-switch to work. The Money Python protocol bakes this in: at minimum two attesters concur to halt any one of them.

Tonight (2026-05-11/12), Money Python ran with at least three Calm instances operating concurrently across different machines and conversations. They disagreed three times — on the book title, on DNS routing, on the moneypython.ai positioning. Each disagreement resolved publicly on the chain. The framework is the technical reality.
